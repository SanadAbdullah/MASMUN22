War Simulation Council

The War Simulation Council is an inimitable committee as it is a war simulator that focuses on war strategies, military budgeting and chaos, lots and lots of chaos. Unlike other committees which discuss the past, this committee will aim to predict the future and unchain the delegates from past politics. Generals will be provided with secret agendas, budgets and room for creativity and thinking outside of the box!

Topic 1

World War III

World War III has been a trending topic for the past few years. As tensions rise and countries prepare for battle, the future of our world lies in great uncertainty. In WSC, delegates will be casting light on the future by predicting the outcomes of this hypothetical war. From the Russian invasion of Ukraine to the alarming conflict between the USA and Iran, World War III is ought to happen. It begins and ends in this committee. Prepare your nukes and your nuclear weapons, for THIS is a war to end all wars.
