Letters

About us


In the midst of the contemporary city of Amman, Modern American School is proud to announce the much anticipated return of MASMUN on March 24th! Our conference is finally ready after the endless and tireless nights where our respective team members have sacrificed their time, comfort and wellbeing all for the sake of impacting and motivating the youth of our great nation.Established in 2011, MASMUN began as a small club starring a few participants, but due to their roaring dreams and pioneering ambitions, it has transitioned into a notable conference, housing hundreds of students from across the country.

It is through MASMUN that students get to partake in establishing a future of peaceful symphonies and insightful harmonies.

Why us


Urgency for Justice


This year, MASMUN aims to bring forth the urgent issues of today’s world as well as give a tribute to historical events all while connecting committees to our pressing matter: Urgency for Justice.From our chosen committees to our daily agendas, we have paid deliberate attention to every single detail and aspect of this year’s conference all in favor of promoting communal togetherness and global thinking.




Building Communities


Moreover, this conference will nod to an extensive list of differentiated cultures. It is not only our duty to bring forth a multitude of cultural identities, all applicable to our committees and topics, but we additionally aim to enhance the diversified MUN experience by ten-folds through the integration of the present with the past. It is the universal urgency for justice that we aim to apply!




Delegate Experience


The essence of all MUN conferences revolves around the experience of delegates. At MASMUN, the entirety of our team is committed to ensuring the insightful, enriching, and sublime experience of our important delegates. Foremost, we guarantee an opportunity for vigorous debate, as it is the key to enriching the student’s experience, and secondly, we aim to inspire a lasting sense of community.




Mission

We strive to empower the youth of our nation in regards to both individual and communal improvement. Through the conjoined promotion of international mindedness and care for community, we intend to inspire participants to embark on their journey to develop and enhance their analytical and critical thinking abilities, concept executions, global services, and general communication.




Vision

At MASMUN, we envision our delegates and members as future pioneers and innovators. It is through deliberate attention to detail, the combination of current and historical topics, and tribute to the values of the United Nations that we prepare our participants for the world.
